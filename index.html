SEEDS seminar
Grad Social, Earth, and Environmental Data Science Seminar  ·  Stanford
What?
A space for junior researchers — graduate students, post-docs, and pre-docs — to discuss quantitative environmental social science at all stages and get feedback in a supportive environment. Pitch an early-stage idea, talk through something you're stuck on, or practice a conference talk.
Who?
Open to grad students, post-docs, and pre-docs across GEP, ESoS, E-IPER, and FSE. Faculty from GEP / ESoS / ESS are invited to every-other meeting. Regularly attending? You're agreeing to present at some point!
When?
Thursdays, 12:00–1:00 pm, every other week (starting February 2026).
Format?
Two 30-min slots per session (sign up for one or both). Each includes time for questions and discussion. Faculty-attended sessions alternate with grad/postdoc-only sessions.
Food?
Working on it — BYO lunch for now.
Organizers
